# Connection Management Strategy

Each connection should fall into one of the following categories.

## Acquisition Partner

Recruiter, Talent Acquisition Partner, Head of People, or HR Manager.

## Executive Leader

C-Level role such as CEO, CTO, CPO, or other executive.

## Technical Leader

L-Level role including Team Lead, Engineering Manager, or Technical Lead.

**Posting Requirements:**

1. 1+ English post in the last 6 months.
2. 3/5 recent posts about project/feature leadership or people management.

## Technical Contributor

Active Software Engineer, Developer, or Architect.

**Posting Requirements:**

1. 1+ English post in the last 6 months.
2. 3/5 recent posts about software development or technical learning.
